# TEH Stationery Shop Knowledge Base — Part 2

## Additional Office Support Services

Beyond supplying essential stationery products, TEH Stationery Shop also provides practical office support services designed to help customers complete their document preparation tasks efficiently. These services are especially useful for students, administrative staff, small businesses, and organizations that require quick and reliable finishing for printed materials.

### Spiral Binding Service (BIND-SP)

The Spiral Binding Service is suitable for customers who need to organize loose documents into a clean, professional format. This service is commonly used for reports, assignments, project documentation, training manuals, proposals, and internal office materials. Spiral binding allows pages to flip easily and lay flat, making it convenient for reading, presenting, and note-taking.

Customers who frequently prepare documents for submission or internal distribution will find this service helpful, especially when dealing with multi-page content that needs durability and neat presentation. The binding process ensures that pages remain secure while still being flexible for daily use.

### A4 Lamination (Per Page) (LAM-A4)

The A4 lamination service is designed to protect printed materials from damage such as water, dust, and frequent handling. It is commonly used for certificates, notices, signage, menus, instructional sheets, and important documents that require long-term durability.

Laminated documents are more resistant to wear and tear, making them suitable for both indoor and light outdoor use. This service is especially useful for schools, offices, and businesses that need reusable materials or documents that must remain in good condition over time.

## Everyday Stationery Add-ons

In addition to core office supplies, TEH Stationery Shop also offers smaller but essential stationery items that support daily operations. These items are often used together with main products and help complete common office or school tasks.

### Max Staples No.10 (Box) (STAPLE-B)

Max Staples No.10 is a reliable choice for fastening documents securely. It is suitable for light to medium stapling needs such as invoices, forms, worksheets, and internal paperwork.

### Post-it Notes 3x3 (STICKY-N)

Post-it Notes are widely used for reminders, quick notes, task tracking, and communication within teams. The 3x3 size is compact yet large enough for writing short messages.

### Clear Cellophane Tape (TAPE-CLR)

Clear cellophane tape is a versatile item used for sealing, packaging, repairing paper, and general office tasks.
