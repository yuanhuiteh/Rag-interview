# TEH Stationery Shop Knowledge Base

## Introduction

TEH Stationery Shop is a stationery shop that supplies practical office and school products for daily printing, writing, filing, and printer refill needs. The shop supports customers such as offices, students, tuition centres, admin departments, finance teams, small businesses, and general users who need reliable stationery for everyday work. The main product categories include A4 copier paper, Pilot G2 gel pens, lever arch files, and Epson EcoTank ink refills.

## Premium A4 Copier Paper

Premium A4 Copier Paper is suitable for printing, photocopying, invoice printing, school worksheets, reports, quotations, forms, and general office documents. The 70gsm A4 paper is a lighter and more cost-friendly choice for daily high-volume printing, while the 80gsm A4 paper has a thicker and more professional feel for formal documents, proposals, resumes, certificates, and client-facing paperwork.

The paper products are available in both box and ream packing. Customers who print frequently or buy for office use can choose box packing, while customers who only need a smaller quantity can choose ream packing. The available SKUs are A4-70G-BOX for 70gsm A4 paper in 1 box with 5 reams, A4-70G-REAM for 70gsm A4 paper in 1 ream, A4-80G-BOX for 80gsm A4 paper in 1 box with 5 reams, and A4-80G-REAM for 80gsm A4 paper in 1 ream.

## Pilot G2 Gel Pen Set

Pilot G2 Gel Pen Set is suitable for smooth writing, signatures, note-taking, form filling, school work, and daily office tasks. The gel ink provides a smoother writing experience compared with many normal ball pens, making it useful for teachers, students, office admins, front desk staff, finance teams, and anyone who writes frequently.

Customers who need formal writing or signature pens can choose the black Pilot G2 set. Customers who need pens for school work, forms, and general writing can choose the blue Pilot G2 set. Customers who need multiple colours for marking, planning, note organization, or visual separation can choose the mixed colour Pilot G2 set. The available SKUs are PEN-G2-BLK for black Pilot G2 gel pens in a box of 12, PEN-G2-BLU for blue Pilot G2 gel pens in a box of 12, and G2-MIX for mixed colour Pilot G2 gel pens in a box of 12.

## Lever Arch File

Lever Arch File is used for storing and organizing documents such as invoices, receipts, contracts, account documents, HR files, student records, admin forms, and project paperwork. The files are black and sold in packs of 10 pieces, making them suitable for office departments, schools, tuition centres, and businesses that need structured document storage.

The 2-inch black lever arch file is suitable for normal filing, monthly reports, invoice records, and medium-volume paperwork. The 3-inch black lever arch file is better for thicker document storage such as yearly accounts, audit files, HR records, and department paperwork with heavier filing needs. The available SKUs are FILE-2IN for 2-inch black lever arch files in 10 pieces and FILE-3IN for 3-inch black lever arch files in 10 pieces.

## Epson EcoTank Ink Refill

Epson EcoTank Ink Refill is available for customers who need printer ink for compatible Epson EcoTank printers. The black ink refill is suitable for regular black-and-white document printing such as invoices, forms, letters, admin paperwork, reports, and internal office records.

The colour combo ink refill is suitable for colour printing such as charts, notices, school materials, visual documents, and presentation handouts. Customers should check printer model compatibility before purchase to make sure the ink matches their Epson EcoTank printer. The available SKUs are INK-BLK for Epson 003 black ink refill and INK-COL for Epson colour combo ink refill containing cyan, magenta, and yellow.

## Shopping Cart and Order Changes

Customers can use the chatbot to manage their shopping cart before checkout. They can add products by saying product names such as "I want A4 paper", "add Pilot pen", or "I need Epson ink". They can also remove items by saying commands such as "remove A4 paper", "buang pen", or "删掉A4纸".

Customers can edit their order by changing quantity, switching variants, adding or removing add-ons, or removing products entirely. For quantity changes, customers can say examples like "change to 5 boxes", "I want 3 reams", "tukar ke 5 kotak", or "改成5盒". If customers want to start again, they can clear the whole cart by saying "reset cart", "clear cart", "kosongkan troli", or "清空购物车".

Customers can view their cart summary and total price at any time. They can ask the chatbot using phrases such as "show cart", "view cart", "how much total", "lihat troli", "berapa jumlah semua", "查看购物车", or "总共多少". The chatbot supports cart actions in English, Bahasa Melayu, and Chinese so customers can manage their orders in the language they are comfortable with.

## Delivery Service
TEH Stationery Shop provides delivery across Peninsular Malaysia, including Klang Valley, Selangor, Kuala Lumpur, Seremban, Negeri Sembilan, Johor, Melaka, Perak, Penang, and other major cities. For Sabah and Sarawak, customers need to contact the team for delivery arrangements and rates.

Standard delivery within Klang Valley takes 1 to 2 working days. Outstation delivery usually takes 3 to 5 working days. Same-day delivery within Klang Valley is available with an additional RM30 fee.

Delivery to Bandar Utama is totally free. For Klang Valley bulk orders above RM500, delivery is also free. For other orders, delivery fees range from RM15 to RM50 depending on location. Delivery to Seremban and Negeri Sembilan is RM25.

## Business Hours and Contact

TEH Stationery Shop is open from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. On Saturday, the shop is open from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The shop is closed on Sundays and Malaysian public holidays.

Customers can contact the sales team through WhatsApp, phone, or email. For immediate assistance, customers can also use the chatbot to request a handoff to the team. Product availability may depend on current stock, so customers can ask about a product name, SKU, variant, packing type, or quantity before placing an order.
